A little about me...

Hi there! My name is Ray (I also go by Hanabihenki, Azurazar, and Raydrawsx). I am a Japanese-American artist with a passion for story telling. I can do 2D and 3D animations, character design, illustrations and 3D models. I create illustrations using Sketchbook and Photoshop and 3D models using Blender.

My strengths in illustrative work are humans, dragons, felines, canines, avians, and anything similar. I can also draw custom/self made species.

I may seem crazy online but in real life I have debilitating social anxiety.
