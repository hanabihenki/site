A little about me...

I am a Japanese-American artist and animator named Ray. Online I go by Hanabihenki, Azurazar, Raydrawsx. Every social media linked here is owned and ran by me. I create illustrations using Sketchbook and Photoshop, 3D models using Blender.

My strengths in illustrative work are humans, dragons, felines, canines, avians, and anything similar.

I can 3D model anything, as long as I put my mind to it! I may seem cray cray online but in real life I'm really quiet.
